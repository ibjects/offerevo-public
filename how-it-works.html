How OfferEvo works

A customer posts the deal. A business gets the visit.

OfferEvo turns local recommendations into simple, shareable discount cards. The flow is designed for people who already love a place and want their friends/followers to try it, hence supporting local businesses.

Step 01

The Pitch

A loyal regular creates a custom discount offer they want to share with their network.

Step 02

The Nod

You get notified. Review the drop details and approve it with a single tap. Zero friction.

Step 03

The Rush

The digital card goes live. They hype it up, and their friends walk through your door.

01

Pick a business

Choose a favorite cafe, barber, bookstore, restaurant, studio, or shop from your local area.

02

Create the offer

Write a clear title, add a short description, pick a color, and preview the card before it goes live.

03

Share with friends

The deal becomes a compact card people can share, save, and use when they visit the shop.

04

Business approves

Businesses can claim their profile, approve offers, and control limits before customers use them.

05

New customers

The strongest channel is still a person saying, “You should try this place.”

06

Everyone learns

Shops see what gets shared, customers see what gets used, and better local offers follow.
