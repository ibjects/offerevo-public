Privacy Policy

This page explains the information OfferEvo expects to collect as we build the platform and how we intend to use it. It is written for clarity, not legal gymnastics.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Information we collect

We may collect contact information you submit through our waitlist forms, such as your name, email, city, business name, or role. As the product develops, we may also collect account details, offer content, business profile information, and usage activity.

How we use information

  • To contact you about OfferEvo access, updates, and onboarding.
  • To understand which cities, neighborhoods, and business categories to prioritize.
  • To operate core product features such as offer creation, business approval, and redemption tracking.
  • To improve safety, reliability, support, and product experience.

Forms and third-party services

Our waitlist forms are powered by HubSpot. When you submit a form, HubSpot may process the information on our behalf according to its own platform terms and privacy practices.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us run the site, communicate with users, analyze usage, or provide customer support.

Your choices

You can ask us to update or remove waitlist information by contacting us at offerevo@outlook.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as OfferEvo moves from early access into a fuller product. The latest version will be posted on this page.
